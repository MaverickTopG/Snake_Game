Snake Game App

Welcome to the Snake Game App, developed by members of AppDev Club! Experience the classic snake game with modern twists and exciting features.

Features

Intuitive Controls

Swipe to control the snake's movement effortlessly.

Dynamic Gameplay

Encounter dynamic obstacles and power-ups.

High Scores

Track your progress and compete for the highest score.

Custom Skins

Personalize your snake with various skins.

Pause & Resume

Pause the game anytime and resume where you left off.

In-Game Instructions

Learn how to play with helpful in-game guidance.
